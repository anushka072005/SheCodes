What is Salsa?

My Favorite Type of Dance

Salsa is a Latin dance, associated with the music genre of the same name, which was first popularized in the United States in the 1960s in New York City. Salsa is a combination of Cuban dances, such as mambo, pachanga, and rumba, as well as American dances such as swing and tap.
